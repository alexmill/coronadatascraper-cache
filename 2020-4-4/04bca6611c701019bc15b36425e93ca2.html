COVID-19 Disease Data

Purpose

To track and monitor COVID-19 disease within the state of Rhode Island.

Key Information

Number of Rhode Island COVID-19 positive and presumptive positive cases, number of people who had negative test results at RIDOH's State Health Laboratory, number of pending test results, and approximate number of people who are currently instructed to self-quarantine in Rhode Island.

Collection Period

February 2020 - current
