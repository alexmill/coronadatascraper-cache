Influenza (Flu) Vaccine Available Here! (while supplies last)

Free Influenza (Flu) Vaccine will be offered to everyone 6 months and older, every Tuesday from 9AM to 12PM  at the Willows Public Health office located at 240 N. Villa Ave. in Willows.

For more information please call (530)934-6588 or you can visit our Immunization Program page.

The flu is a contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses that infect the nose, throat, and lungs. It can cause mild to severe illness, and at times can lead to hospitalization or even death. Annual vaccination for the flu is the best method to prevent or lessen the effects from the flu.
