Current status

The World Health Organisation (WHO) has announced that COVID-19 is a pandemic.

Currently, Australia does not have widespread community transmission of COVID-19. Find out how we are managing the outbreak to slow the spread.

In Australia

As at 6:30am on 28 March 2020, there have been 3,378 confirmed cases of COVID-19 in Australia. There have been 212 new cases since 3:00pm yesterday.

Of the 3,378 confirmed cases in Australia, 13 have died from COVID-19
